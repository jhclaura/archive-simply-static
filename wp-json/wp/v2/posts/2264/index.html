    • instead of depicting objects from one viewpoint, the artist depicts the subject from a multitude of viewpoints<\/span><\/li>\n

      Dada<\/strong><\/p>\n

        \n
      • started around 1910<\/li>\n
      • thinks the logic and reason of bourgeois caused the WWI, so\u00a0express the rejection by embracing chaos and illogic<\/li>\n

      • a systematic work of destruction and demoralization<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

        Surrealism<\/strong><\/p>\n

          \n
        • 1920\u2019s<\/li>\n
        • resolve the previously contradictory conditions of dream and reality<\/li>\n

        • Freud’s work with free association, dream analysis, and the unconscious<\/li>\n
        • aimed to revolutionize human experience<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

            \n

            { Now }<\/h2>\n

            Frankly, I can’t define what’s the Nonsense is nowaday. But below are what I found related:<\/p>\n

            Arty\u00a0Narcissism<\/strong><\/p>\n

            “…pretentious, meaningless, tongue twisting nonsense spoken in some artists\u2019 statements. The culprits are mainly the conceptual artists who need to bamboozle you with an attempt at philosophical genius in order to camouflage the insignificance of the poorly executed end product.<\/em>“<\/p>\n

            “The contemporary market has been subject to extreme speculation, but there is a difference between speculation and fraud. The economic downturn will moderate the speculative bubble that has seen some less deserving artists command extreme prices.”<\/em><\/p>\n

            Justwalkingism<\/strong><\/p>\n

            “A game that you just walk in.\u00a0It\u2019s not about winning, it\u2019s not about overcoming the challenges, it\u2019s not even about challenges anymore\u2026 Justwalkingism is giving you something to go through and what is important is that you actually go through it. You.”<\/em><\/p>\n

            “Justwalkingism is\u00a0an art movement, meaning that its artists express themselves on many platforms. It’s\u00a0like colouring books: the outlines are there but the finished piece is still yours to make.”<\/em><\/p>\n
