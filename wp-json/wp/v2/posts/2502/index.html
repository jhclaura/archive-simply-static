Yesterday I was chatting with\u00a0a friend about how to identify ourselves\u00a0– are we\u00a0programmer, designer, maker, artist?<\/p>\n

He said he was not and never will be an artist. He wants to make things that solve problems.<\/p>\n

And I said “But my projects never really solved a problem. They are mostly fun and weird and just bring laughs to people. So I’m just generating useless and needless garbage to the world?”<\/p>\n

The world is already flooded with media garbage and I’m just one of the contributor?<\/p>\n

The thoughts depressed me for a few minutes, but that’s it.<\/p>\n

I think I can just keep making something that amplifies the unnoticeable and stirs people’s minds. And maybe it would solve someone’s problem. Who knows? There are 7.4 billion people on the earth. And there are also animals. And there is\u00a0also consciousness existing in the interstellar…<\/p>\n
