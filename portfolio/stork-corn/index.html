Stork Corn is a project of  SMOG/CORN, a digital magazine of Ben Kauffman and Sarah Rothberg. It’s about stork, corn, yourself as kernels, and decision.

Click to join. Made with Three.js, WebRTC, and WebSocket. Click to aim. ←↑→↓ to walk. Space to explode. Each user is represented as a red stork, and all lives and interact with this world that made out of corns.
